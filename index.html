    AWS SaaS Community¶

    Are you an influencer or disrupter in your community, organization, or across the broader SaaS industry? Team up with AWS and fellow execs and peers to help boost a next generation of innovative developers, thinking, and methodologies for the quickly evolving world of SaaS.

    How to get involved¶

    We welcome an SaaS Builder to join the conversation, but we are recruiting community leaders as well. If you already belong to an AWS Community program, like the AWS Community Builders, AWS Partner Ambassadors, or AWS Heroes, and are interested in helping lead the conversation on SaaS, reach out and let us know.
